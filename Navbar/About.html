Our Story

Founded in 2019, CraftConnect emerged from a simple belief: that handmade items carry a soul that mass-produced goods simply cannot match. Our founders, Sarah and Michael, were themselves artisans who struggled to find the right platform to showcase their work.

What started as a weekend project in their garage has grown into a thriving marketplace that connects thousands of artisans with customers who value authenticity, sustainability, and craftsmanship.

Today, we're proud to support over 2,500 artisans worldwide, helping them turn their passion into sustainable businesses while bringing unique, meaningful products to homes everywhere.
